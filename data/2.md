### rust ssh lib 

除加密库之外纯 **rust** 实现的 **ssh client** 库 **[ssh-rs](https://github.com/1148118271/ssh-rs)** ，加密库依赖 **[ring](https://github.com/briansmith/ring)**，**[rust-openssl](https://github.com/sfackler/rust-openssl)**。
